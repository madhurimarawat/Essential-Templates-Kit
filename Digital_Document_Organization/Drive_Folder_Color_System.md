# 🎨 Drive Folder Color System

A structured color-coding system to keep all files organized, fast to access, and easy to maintain.

📌 Hierarchy idea: (This is for rest of inner folders, outer folder will have the darkest color)

---

## 🔴 Red — Important (High Priority)

For critical and urgent items.

**Includes:**

* Critical documents, recovery keys, system description
* Urgent tasks
* High-priority files
* Time-sensitive work
* Exam next stage documents and admit cards

---

## 🔵 Blue — Shared / Collaboration

For anything involving sharing or teamwork.

**Includes:**

* Shared files
* Collaborative projects
* Group work folders
* Reference materials used in projects
* Shared learning resources

📌 Example: Internship certificates publicly shared, etc.

📌 Hierarchy idea:

* Dark Blue → Active shared projects
* Medium Blue → Ongoing collaboration
* Light Blue → Old references / rarely used shared docs

---

## 🟢 Green — Academic Documents

All education-related and official academic records.

**Includes:**

* Semester results
* Certificates (all academic certificates)
* College documents
* Marksheets and exam records

📌 Hierarchy idea:

* Light Green → Older Results / School Results 10 - 12
* Medium Green → Middle Degree Btech
* Dark Green → Latest degree MA results / most recent academics

📌 All college-related certificates + Degree / Result documents stay inside Green system only.

---

## 🟤 Brown — Temporary / Short-Term Important

For documents needed only for a limited time.

**Includes:**

* Admit cards (especially final/second-stage exams)
* Travel tickets
* Hotel bookings
* Exam-related temporary documents

📌 Delete or archive after use.

📌 Hierarchy idea:

- Light Brown: Older exams who results are already out and score card out too.
- Medium Brown: Results out scorecard pending.
- Dark Brown: Results Pending

---

## 🟣 Purple — Current Job / Active Professional Docs

For everything related to your **ongoing job or active professional engagement**.

**Includes:**

* Current job offer/appointment documents
* Joining letters
* Work-related certificates
* Office address documents
* ID proofs used for job
* HR documents
* Active employment paperwork

📌 This is ONLY for current job-related material (not job search or applications).

📌 Hierarchy idea:

* Light Purple → Old job-related drafts / past onboarding docs
* Medium Purple → Active job documents in use
* Dark Purple → Most critical current employment files (HR, ID, official proof)
