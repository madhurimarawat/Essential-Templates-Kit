# ♻️ Reusing-Repurposing-Old-Photocopies-Papers-Photos-Boxes

So we all have piles of old stuff, docs, papers, photos, boxes and what do we do with them? Throw them away. So here I would give you some nice personal ideas that I use myself.

## 📄 Old Photocopies

The first and foremost thing is that after some time photocopies become useless. So instead of throwing them away, use them like one side paper to make notes or do writing stuff. If you do not want to do any DIY, just staple your old copies and use them like a mini notebook.

### ✂️ DIY

#### 1. 📑 Bookmarks

1. Cut your photocopy into 4 equal parts and paste these parts one on one. Cut the corners rounded.
2. Color it if you want or not.
3. Write your favorite quotes or make a doodle. You can also have categories like I do, horror creepy quotes like "you are not alone", "someone is seeing you", "sins of darkness".
4. After this, punch a hole at the top and tie any wool thread or ribbons or even shoelaces if you have new ones. They do a really good job.
5. You can laminate with cello tape too.
6. Do not try to make it as good as market or readymade ones. It will be unique, personal and you. Focus on that personal touch. You can further write whatever makes you feel good in it, your favorite book or the book you are reading.

#### 2. 🗒️ Sticky Notes

1. Super underrated use, really very simple, just cut in whatever shape you want.
2. Paste one on one using Fevistick and you got your own version.
3. I do this all the time as you can have your favorite shapes like heart, circle or code logo (<>).
4. To cut in equal parts, use this trick. Fold the paper in the number of folds possible, then cut it. All pieces will be the same.
5. For next copies, just keep one of the already cut pieces and you are good to go.

#### 3. 📓 Pocket Diary

1. Just make small portions of copies, staple them or punch them and tie a thread and you got your own diary.
2. For the outer cover, use any old copy cover or leave it as it is. Either way it is for personal use.

## 🖼️ Old Photos

Keep an A4 size paper, cut it into fourths, postcard size. Paste all your old passport size photos and you get a picture gallery in your album. Especially use old photos 6 to 7 or even 10 years older.
